Lion cubs play-fight to learn hunting skills. Rats play to learn social & emotional skills. Monkeys play to learn cognitive, creative, and improvisational skills.

And yet — in the last century, we humans have somehow convinced ourselves that 1) play is a waste of time, and 2) learning must be boring. Golly, no wonder we’re all so miserable.

Welcome to Explorable Explanations, a hub for learning complex ideas through play! We’re a totally unorganized “movement” of artists, programmers, and educators who care about active learning.
